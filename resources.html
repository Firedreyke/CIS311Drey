Additional Resources

Here is a quick list of some of my favorite cybersecurity resources!

VirusTotal
VirusTotal is a great resource for quickly assessing threats; you can upload suspicious files, check if a URL is malicious, or search for almost any other criteria!
urlscan.io
Urlscan.io is used to view webpages without actually resolving to them. Urlscan will show you a preview of the page, view its code, and provide a classification if the page seems malicious.

Note: Please be wary of uploading links you may wish to keep secret; links searched for with urlscan are not completely private.
ipinfo.io
Ipinfo can be used to gather research about a given IP address, and possibly help determine if it is malicious. I have used it frequently for looking up the sending servers of emails reported as phishing, to pinpoint where they are actually coming from.

Note: This tool can only be used a few times a day before an account is required.
